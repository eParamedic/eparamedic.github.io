eParamedic

You never know when an injury could occur. Having the option to be able to call for help in your home is invaluable and has the ability to save lives by getting help when the person would normally be unable to.

Easy to use

Unlike most apps, this service can be used at any time and does not require any extra experience other than knowing eParamedic exists.

Instantaneous

Unlike asking someone else to google what to do, eParamedic tells you exactly what to do right there and then.

Calming

In the case of an emergency, it is helpful to have someone there guiding you and calming you so that you can think clearly and help in the best way possible.

A bit about the process

When eParamedic is invoked through a Google Assistant or Amazon Alexa, was created using Voiceflow. It will ask for the emergency and its details in order to recommend steps to take through the safety and emergency database we designed. The user can then ask the device for help and guidance on how to approach the situation at hand.
